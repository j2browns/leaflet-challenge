USGS Map of EarthQuakes in US

From 2019-01-01 till 2019-01-07

The above map shows earthquakes in the US between Jan 1 and 7th of 2019.
The size of the circle indicates the intensity or strength of the earthquake. The color indicates the depth as shown in the legend in the lower right corner.
A pop up for each location is also available if you click on the point.
Also shown on the map is the location of the tectonic plates (yellow lines). The map style can be changed between a street map and a satellite view.
The user can also turn on and off layers showing the earthquake locations and tectonic plates.
